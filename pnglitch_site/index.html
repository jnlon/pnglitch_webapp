Intro

PNGlitch is webapp that "glitches" uploaded PNG image files. This is a school project that was inspired by the The Art of PNG Glitch.

There is a form at the bottom of this page where you can upload any valid PNG image smaller than 10MB.

FAQ

Does it only work with PNG image files?

At the moment, yes.

Why does it freeze when I upload my image?

This probably means that the server is busy. You can try waiting it out or uploading again in a few minutes.

What happens to my images after I upload them?

After uploading, your image will be glitched, then the glitched images are saved temorarily on the server to that you can download them. They are deleted automatically after about a minute.
